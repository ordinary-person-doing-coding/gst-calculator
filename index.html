What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive indirect tax in India. For intrastate sales, it's split into CGST (Central) and SGST (State). For interstate, it's IGST (Integrated). Common rates: 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%. Use this tool for quick estimates—consult a tax professional for official advice.
